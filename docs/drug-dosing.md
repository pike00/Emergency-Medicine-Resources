# Drug Dosing

## Intubation

### Pre-treatment
| Medication | Dose (70kg) | Calculation |
|------------|-------------|-------------|
| Lidocaine  | 105 mg      | 1.5 mg/kg   |
| Fentanyl   | 70 mcg      | 1  mcg/kg   |
