## Analgesia
- Tylenol
- Flexeril

## Antibiotics
- Amoxicillin, Augmentin
- Penicillin
- Macrobid
- Erythromycin
- Keflex

## Asthma
- Isoproterenol
- Albuterol
- Beclomethasone
- Cromolyn

## Cold Remedies
- Pseudoephedrine (in **2nd and 3rd** trimesters only)
- Guanifensin (Robitussin)
- Vicks Vapor rub
- Dephenhydramine
- Throat Lozenges (any brand)
- Oxymetolazine (Afrin; For a maximum of 3 days)

## Allergies
- Cetirizine
- Loratidine

## Constipation
- Milk of Magnesia (Magnesium Hydroxide)
- Docusate (Colace)
- Fiber laxatives e.g. Metamucil

## Diarrhea
- Bismuth Salicylate
- Imodium (In **2nd and 3rd** trimesters only

## Hemorrhoids
- Proctofoam
- Preparation H
- Anusol
- Tucks

## Indigestion
- Tums
- Maalox
- Rolaids
- Mylanta
- Riopan plus simethicone
- Pepcid
- Zantac
- Tagamet

# Nausea & Vomiting
- Emetrol
- Gatorade
- Reglan
- Compazine
- Phenergan
- Zofran ODT
- Dramamine
- Meclizine
- Vitamin B6
- Peppermint and ginger tea
